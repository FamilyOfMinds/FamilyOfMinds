The System

Three things shared memory does for your team

01 — Institutional Memory

Context that doesn't walk out the door

Every decision is logged and searchable. When a key person leaves, their knowledge stays. When you onboard someone new, they have everything.

02 — Causal Provenance

Trace any decision back to its origin

Ask "why did we make this call six months ago?" and get an actual answer. Every note connects to what caused it and what it led to.

03 — Agent Coordination

AI tools that know what happened last week

Your agents share context across sessions and tools. No more re-explaining. No more context collapse. They wake up already knowing.
